description: >
  Bots on Little Golem
---

Since January 1, 2026, bots are allowed on Little Golem only in casual games started by a human challenge.

The permitted bots are listed on the [bots page](https://www.littlegolem.net/jsp/bots/index.jsp), where players can start a game with them directly. Bots also play regular tournaments among themselves so that players can compare their strength.

Bots are not allowed in regular human tournaments. Modern game engines can be stronger than human players in many board games, so tournament play is reserved for human accounts unless a tournament is explicitly marked as a bot event.
