This site documents Little Golem games, tournaments, ratings and account rules.

# Overview

LittleGolem.net was founded in 2000. It is a turn-based site for abstract board games such as chess, shogi, go, draughts, hex and many modern connection, word and strategy games.

Games are usually played as day games. Each player starts with a 10-day time bank and receives 36 hours after each move, up to the time-bank limit.

Most games are played in [tournaments](/tournaments/), but players can also use the waiting room, invitations and bot challenges.
